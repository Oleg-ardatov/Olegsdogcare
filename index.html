Welcome to Oleg's Dog Walking Services!

At Oleg's Dog Walking Services, every visit is handled with care, professionalism, and full preparation. Each walk is equipped with all the essentials to ensure your dog’s safety, comfort, and happiness. Walks are always conducted with eco-friendly poop bags, and a leash can be provided if necessary. Fresh water and high-quality treats (available as additional paid services) can also be provided upon request. Feeding services are carried out with attention to hygiene and accurate portioning. IF YOUR DOG HAS ANY MEDICAL ISSUES OR DIETARY RESTRICTIONS, PLEASE INFORM US IN ADVANCE so your dog can be fed safely and appropriately. Oleg's offers dependable, personal, and compassionate care that treats every pet like family—making this service the trusted choice for your beloved companion.
